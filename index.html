Storms Happen—
Outages Don’t Have to with SolaX

Keep your home and all appliances powered during an outage. When severe weather is forecast, Storm Watch will automatically charge your SolaX system to its maximum capacity in preparation. During an outage, SolaX will help keep your solar system running, or if you're using grid power, it will seamlessly transition your home to stored energy instantly. Additionally, with our app, you can manage your energy from the cloud—store, sell, or use it as needed.
